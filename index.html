About me

Hello peeps,
I'm a data science post-graduate from The University of Adelaide. My research areas are Generative Adeversarial Networks (GANs) and I am focusing on Unpaired Image to Image translation using GANs working under supervision of Dr Rafael Felix Alves, AIML. Also, working on Natural Language Processing (Text and Audio).
Between these studies I worked as a summer research scholar for Centre for Research on Engineering Software Technologies (CREST, The University of Adelaide) and Cyber Security Cooperative Research Centre (CSCRC) and developed a novel methodology of automated security patch classification.

Teaching

As a part-time, I also worked as teaching assistant (2020 - Present) for few subjects which involves: Programming (Matlab and C), Software Engineering Research Project, Introduction to Software Engineering, Big Data and Analysis, Object Oriented Programming. I was also an International Peer Study Support Mentor in 2020.

Furthermore, I did my bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering from India and worked with Indian Navy, Defence Laboratory and IBM with parallel to my studies.
